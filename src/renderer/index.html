i Auto-sync measures each speaker for you. It plays a short test chirp on every ticked speaker — local ones, your ticked Sonos rooms and AirPlay receivers — one at a time, and listens with your Mac’s microphone to time how long each takes to be heard. It then delays every speaker to match the slowest (often Sonos or an AirPlay TV): locals via their Delay comp, Sonos & AirPlay via their group delay.

Tips: put the mic near your listening spot, keep the room quiet, and use the built-in mic (not BlackHole). Sonos takes a few extra seconds to measure. You can still fine-tune by ear afterward.
